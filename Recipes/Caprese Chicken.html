Caprese Chicken

Juicy Mozzarella, Tomato, and Basil Stuffed Chicken Breasts

This mouth-watering seared and baked chicken breast will leave anyone impressed.

It's got everyhing in it to make a perfect meal! Stringy cheese, flavorful Kumatos, fresh Basil, and the best Sun Dried Tomatos

Ingredients:

  • Chicken Breast
  • Fresh Basil
  • Garlic
  • Sun Dried Tomatos
  • Kumatos (or your choice of tomato
  • Mozzarella

Steps:

  1. Preheat oven to 400
  2. Dry chicken breasts
  3. Cut pocket into chicken
  4. Stuff with Ingredients
  5. Sear medium-high 5 minutes on each side
  6. Top with sun dried tomatoes and bake in the oven for 13 minutes at 400 degrees
  7. Let rest for a few minutes then serve
